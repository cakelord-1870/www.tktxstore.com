How to Use TKTX:
1.Wash the area receiving tattoo or laser treatment with soap and water. 
2.Cover a Hot Towel on skin for 5 Mintues , Dry area completely.
3.Apply a thick amount of TKTX numbing cream to the area and rub in thoroughly.
4.After rubbing into the skin, apply a 2nd heavy layer of TKTX cream (1/8" or 2-3 mm thick) over 
the area.
5.Cover the TKTX cream with plastic wrap (saran wrap) and keep warm. The heat under the plastic 
wrap helps activate the cream and keeps the cream from drying out.
6.Leave TKTX cream and wrap in place for 25-50 Minutes before tattoo. For long procedures lasting hours 
over large areas; apply numbing cream over the entire work area, then remove in sections as 
procedure progresses.
7.Skin typically remains numb for 3 - 5 hours once cream is removed from numbed skin depending on 
skin type and location on body.

Warning:
Store above 0°C and below 30°C.

Keep out of reach children.
